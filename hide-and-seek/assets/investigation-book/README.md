# Investigation Book — Rural Hide and Seek

In Jet Lag: Hide and Seek, Seekers use an **investigation book** — a list of yes/no geographic questions they can ask the Hiders to narrow down their location. This is not a card deck; the questions are pre-printed in a booklet and the Seekers choose which to ask.

Some questions in the investigation book are locked until **end game** is declared.
