## Design Philosophy

The goal is to preserve what makes Hide and Seek tense and fun — the seeker closing in question by question, the hider's careful positioning — while replacing transit infrastructure with what rural players actually have: county roads and a personal vehicle.

Key changes from the urban format:
- Transit zones → driving-time isochrones
- Transit passes → Range Cards (vehicle-agnostic mileage budget)  
- Subway/bus stop questions → county, highway, and landmark-based questions
- City-block scale → county-road scale (50+ mile radius)
