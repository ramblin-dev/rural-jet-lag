# Hide and Seek — Rural Variant

An unofficial fan adaptation of **Jet Lag: The Game — Hide and Seek** for rural environments where public transit does not exist.

> **Disclaimer:** This is an unofficial fan project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Jet Lag: The Game, Nebula, or Wendover Productions. All rights to the original game concept belong to their respective owners.

---

## How It Works

One team (**Hiders**) picks a secret location anywhere in the agreed play area and hides there. The other team (**Seekers**) starts from a central point and must physically locate the Hiders' vehicle before time runs out.

Seekers spend **Location Cards** to ask yes/no geographic questions that narrow down where the Hiders are. Hiders spend **Evasion Cards** to dodge questions, force detours, or buy silence. Both teams manage a **Range Card** budget that limits how far they can drive.

The play area is scaled to rural distances — typically a 50-mile radius across county roads — rather than a transit map.

## Quick Reference

- **Play area:** 25–100 mile radius; county lines or named roads as boundaries
- **Travel:** Personal vehicle on public roads
- **Range budget:** Each team starts with Range Cards totaling ~400 miles of driving
- **Seeker tool:** Location Cards — ask a yes/no question about the Hider's geographic position
- **Hider tool:** Evasion Cards — refuse a question, misdirect, or lock down Seekers temporarily
- **Win condition:** Seekers find the vehicle before time expires; Hiders survive the clock
