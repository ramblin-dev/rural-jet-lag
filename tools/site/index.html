How to upload to Google My Maps
  1. Open mymaps.google.com and click Create a new map.
  2. Under the default layer, click Import and drop your downloaded KML file.
  3. Each station becomes a pin. The whole import is one layer — delete it in one click after the game.
  4. Share the map with the other team so seekers and the hider can see the stations.

To start over, delete the play-area polygon with the trash tool on the map. Settings persist.
