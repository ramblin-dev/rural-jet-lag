# Rural Challenge Database

A crowdsourced list of rural-specific challenges for use in Jet Lag: Hide and Seek games.

> **Contributing:** Open a pull request to add your challenges. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](../CONTRIBUTING.md).

---

## Challenge Format

Each challenge follows this format:

> **Name** | Type | Difficulty  
> *Description of what the player must do.*  
> **Reward:** What the completing team gets.

---

## 🏘️ Small Town Challenges

**Smallest Town in the County** | Navigation | Medium  
*Drive to and photograph the welcome sign (or post office) of the smallest incorporated municipality in your current county. Verify population using Census.gov or Wikipedia.*  
**Reward:** +20 minutes of game time.

---

**One-Stoplight Town** | Navigation | Easy  
*Enter any incorporated town with exactly one traffic signal. Photograph the light.*  
**Reward:** Draw 1 free card.

---

**Ghost Town** | Navigation | Hard  
*Find and photograph a formerly incorporated or platted town that no longer has a functioning post office, school, or city government. Provide the town name.*  
**Reward:** +Half Tank.

---

**Town Population Under 100** | Navigation | Medium  
*Find and enter any incorporated place with a population under 100 people according to the most recent Census.*  
**Reward:** Draw 1 Powerup Card.

---

**Double-Digit ZIP Code Town** | Trivia/Navigation | Hard  
*Name and drive to any town in the play area that has a 5-digit ZIP code where all digits are the same or sequential (e.g., 77777 or 12345). Only valid if such a ZIP code exists within 100 miles of the play area.*  
**Reward:** +Half Tank + Draw 1 free card.

---

**Town Named After a Person** | Trivia | Easy  
*Name any town in the play area that was named after a historical individual. State the person's name and why the town was named for them.*  
**Reward:** +Quarter Tank.

---

## 🛣️ Road & Navigation Challenges

**County Road Number** | Navigation | Easy  
*Photograph a county road sign (e.g., "CR 205," "FM 1340," or "CH-7").*  
**Reward:** +Quarter Tank.

---

**Named Gravel Road** | Navigation | Easy  
*Find a gravel or unpaved road with a posted name (not just a number). Photograph the sign.*  
**Reward:** +Quarter Tank.

---

**Dead End** | Navigation | Easy  
*Drive to any dead-end road and photograph the dead-end or no-outlet sign.*  
**Reward:** +Quarter Tank.

---

**Named Creek or River Crossing** | Navigation | Easy  
*Cross any named creek or river on a bridge. Name it (visible on the bridge or on a map).*  
**Reward:** +Quarter Tank.

---

**Covered Bridge** | Navigation | Hard  
*Find and cross any covered bridge within 100 miles of the play area. (Verify it is a genuine historic covered bridge, not a decorative one.)*

---

**Highest Accessible Point** | Navigation | Hard

**Reward:** Draw 1 Powerup Card.

---

**Four-Way Stop at County Lines** | Navigation | Hard  
*Find any intersection where four county roads from four different counties meet. Photograph.*  
**Reward:** +Half Tank.

---

**State Line** | Navigation | Medium  
*Drive to and photograph a state line welcome sign. (Only valid if a state line is within the play area boundary.)*  
**Reward:** Draw 1 Powerup Card.

---

**Speed Limit Drop to 25** | Navigation | Easy  
*Enter a town where the posted speed limit drops to 25 mph or lower. Photograph the speed limit sign.*

## 🤝 Social Challenges

**Local Recommendation** | Social | Easy  
*Ask any local business employee or resident to recommend their favorite place to eat, visit, or see in the county. Show proof (photo of the conversation or a voice note).*  
**Reward:** Draw 1 free card.

---

**Directions from a Local** | Social | Medium  
*Ask a local (in person) for directions to a specific landmark without using a phone. Follow their directions. Show proof of arrival.*  
**Reward:** +Quarter Tank.

---

**Church Count** | Social/Navigation | Easy  
*Count and report the total number of churches (any denomination) in any single small town you visit. Verify by driving through the entire town.*  
**Reward:** +Quarter Tank.

---

**Local Event** | Social | Hard  
*Find and attend any local community event (farmers market, livestock auction, town festival, county fair, etc.) for at least 15 minutes. Show proof.*  
**Reward:** +Half Tank + Draw 1 free card.

---

## 📝 Notes

- All challenges must be completed safely and legally.
- Do not trespass on private property.
- "Photograph" means a timestamped photo shareable via your team's group chat.
- "Voice note" means an audio recording shared with all players.
- Any disputed challenge can be resolved by a majority vote of all players.
