## Design Philosophy

The core goal is to preserve the **spirit** of Jet Lag: Hide and Seek — the tension of a seeker closing in, the hider's need for clever positioning, and the role of luck from challenge cards — while replacing the transit infrastructure with what rural players actually have: county roads and a tank of gas.
