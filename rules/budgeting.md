# Budgeting Mechanics

> Part of the [Rural Jet Lag Ruleset](./README.md)

---

## Core Change: Transit Passes → Gas Cards

In urban Hide and Seek, players spend a budget on transit tickets and passes. In the rural variant, **fuel is the primary resource**. Players draw and spend **Gas Cards** to move, and must manage their tank carefully.

---

## Gas Card System

### Card Values
Each Gas Card represents a fuel allowance. Suggested denominations:

| Card | Value |
|------|-------|
| Quarter Tank | ~100 miles of driving |
| Half Tank | ~200 miles of driving |
| Full Tank | ~400 miles of driving |
| Jerrycan | +50 miles (bonus card, awarded by challenges) |

Adjust mileage values to match your vehicle's real-world fuel economy.

### Starting Allocation
Each team begins the game with cards totaling **one full tank** (approximately 400 miles).

- This is enough to explore a 50–100 mile play area for 4–6 hours.
- For longer games or larger areas, increase starting allocation to **1.5–2 full tanks**.

### Spending Gas
- Before driving any leg, the active team announces the destination.
- Deduct the estimated mileage from their current Gas Card balance.
- If a team runs out of gas mid-game, they must draw a **Breakdown Card** (see below) or forfeit that turn.

---

## Cost Adjustments

### Why Mileage Instead of Dollars?
Fuel prices fluctuate. Using mileage rather than dollar amounts keeps the game fair regardless of when or where you play. If you prefer dollars, set a **baseline gas price** at the start of the game (e.g., the price posted at the nearest gas station) and convert cards to dollar values.

### Vehicle Wear & Tear (Optional Rule)
For multi-day games, introduce a **Maintenance Token** system:
- Each team starts with 3 Maintenance Tokens.
- Driving on gravel or unpaved roads costs 1 token per 10 miles.
- Running out of tokens means the team must "visit a mechanic" (sit out one turn).

---

## Budget Events

These events can be triggered by challenge cards or scenario cards:

| Event | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **Gas Bonus** | Draw one free Gas Card |
| **Price Spike** | All future Gas Cards cost double until the next challenge |
| **Fuel Spill** | Lose a Half Tank immediately |
| **Free Fill-Up** | Seeker loses a Half Tank (hider found a free charging/fueling station) |
| **Breakdown** | Sit out for 10 game-minutes; draw a Quarter Tank to continue |
| **Carpool** | Two teams must travel together for the next 20 game-minutes |

---

## Example Budget Calculation

**Play area:** 3 counties, roughly 60-mile radius  
**Game duration:** 5 hours  
**Estimated driving per team:** 150–250 miles  
**Recommended starting allocation:** Half Tank + Quarter Tank (300 miles total)
