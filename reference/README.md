# Reference — cross-game research

Background research that supports the cross-game mechanics in this repo (currently just [`../vehicle-stations.md`](../vehicle-stations.md)). Per-game reference material — original-rulebook structural notes, card inventories, wiki imports — lives under `<game>/reference/`, not here.

## What's here

- [`transit-friction.md`](./transit-friction.md) — synthesis of academic research on the friction transit imposes on trip-making (waiting, transferring, route topology, last-mile walks, mode-speed gaps, stop spacing, density thresholds), with the rural-design implications that follow. Underpins the wait-time tiers, the cluster-spacing parameters, and the arrival-cooldown rule in `vehicle-stations.md`.

## Writing posture

Per the repo's [`CLAUDE.md`](../CLAUDE.md): per-citation claims are facts from the cited paper; the framing and the rural-design implications are reasoned inference, marked as such. Speculation is dropped or labeled. No verbatim text from the official Jet Lag rulebooks appears here.
