## Challenge Cards

Challenge cards can be played by either team. The opposing team completes the challenge for a reward. If they refuse or fail, the playing team gains the benefit instead.

| # | Card Name | Challenge Text | Reward |
|---|-----------|----------------|--------|
| 1 | **Water Tower Sighting** | Find and photograph a water tower with the town's name on it. | +Quarter Tank |
| 2 | **General Store Stop** | Buy at least one item from a locally owned general store or feed store. Show receipt. | +1 Challenge Card |
| 3 | **Grain Elevator** | Drive to and photograph the nearest grain elevator. | +Quarter Tank |
| 4 | **Historical Marker** | Find any roadside historical marker and read it aloud on a voice note. | Swap 1 card with opponent's hand |
| 5 | **Smallest Town** | Drive to the smallest incorporated municipality in your current county. Verify population via Wikipedia or Census.gov. | +20 minutes of game time |
| 6 | **Post Office** | Photograph the exterior of any US Post Office with its ZIP code visible. | Draw 1 Powerup Card |
| 7 | **County Seat** | Name the county seat of any county in the play area not currently occupied by any team. | Draw 1 free card |
| 8 | **Volunteer Fire Station** | Photograph any volunteer fire department (VFD) sign or station. | +Quarter Tank |
| 9 | **Gravel Road Mile** | Drive at least 1 mile on an unpaved road. Show GPS track or odometer change. | +Quarter Tank |
| 10 | **Farm Stand** | Buy produce or goods from a roadside farm stand. | +1 Curse Card |
| 11 | **State Highway Number** | Photograph yourself at a highway sign with a number below 30. | +Quarter Tank |
| 12 | **Livestock Sighting** | Photograph at least 10 head of cattle or sheep visible from a public road. | +1 Challenge Card |
| 13 | **Bridge Crossing** | Cross any named bridge (name must appear on a map). Say the bridge name out loud on a voice note. | +Quarter Tank |
| 14 | **Town Population Sign** | Photograph a town population sign (the kind at city limits). | Draw 1 Powerup Card |
| 15 | **County Road Number** | Photograph a county road sign (e.g., "CR 205" or "FM 1340"). | +Quarter Tank |
| 16 | **Roadside Oddity** | Find and photograph anything listed on Roadtrippers, Atlas Obscura, or Roadsideamerica.com within the play area. | +Half Tank |
| 17 | **Local Diner** | Enter a locally owned diner or café (not a chain). Order at least a coffee or drink. | Draw 1 free card |
| 18 | **Church Count** | Count and report the number of churches within any single small town you visit. | +1 Challenge Card |
| 19 | **Elevation Change** | Find the highest point accessible by public road in any county in the play area. Photograph. | Draw 1 Powerup Card |
| 20 | **Creek or River** | Cross any named creek or river. Name it. | +Quarter Tank |
| 21 | **Speed Limit Drop** | Enter a town where the speed limit drops to 25 mph or lower. Photograph the sign. | +Quarter Tank |
| 22 | **Named Gravel Road** | Find a gravel road with a posted name (not just a number). Photograph the sign. | +Quarter Tank |
| 23 | **Silo Row** | Find three or more grain silos visible simultaneously from a public road. Photograph. | +Quarter Tank |
| 24 | **Dead End** | Drive to a dead-end road. Photograph the dead-end sign. | +Quarter Tank |
| 25 | **One-Stoplight Town** | Enter a town with exactly one traffic light. | Draw 1 free card |
| 26 | **Abandoned Building** | Photograph any clearly abandoned building visible from a public road. | +Quarter Tank |
| 27 | **Gas Price Check** | Report the per-gallon gas price at any gas station in the play area. | +Quarter Tank if the price is above $3.50; +Half Tank if above $4.00 |
| 28 | **Local Radio** | Tune to an AM or FM radio station originating in the play area. Name the station and call letters. | +Quarter Tank |
| 29 | **Farm Equipment** | Photograph farm equipment actively being used in a field (from a public road). | Draw 1 Powerup Card |
| 30 | **Covered Bridge** | Find and cross a covered bridge within the play area. | +Half Tank + Draw 1 free card |

## Powerup Cards (Hider Team)

| # | Card Name | Effect |
|---|-----------|--------|
| 1 | **Ghost Mode** | Seekers cannot ask about your location or play Curse Cards for 20 minutes. |
| 2 | **Decoy** | Announce a false location (name a specific town). Seekers must drive there before receiving any more location hints. |
| 3 | **Fuel Drain** | Seekers lose a Quarter Tank immediately. |
| 4 | **Smallest Town Shield** | While in the smallest incorporated municipality of the play area, seekers need an extra Quarter Tank to narrow your location. |
| 5 | **Road Block** | Name a specific road. Seekers may not use that road for 20 minutes. |
| 6 | **Signal Scramble** | Seekers may not ask locals for information for 15 minutes. |
| 7 | **County Lock** | Seekers must stay in their current county for 20 minutes. |
| 8 | **Free Crossing** | Cross any county or state line without spending a Crossing Card. |
| 9 | **Extra Time** | Add 20 minutes to the game clock (hider benefit). |
| 10 | **Mirage** | Play at any time. When seekers think they've spotted you, reveal that you left that location 30+ minutes ago (only valid if true). |

## Curse Cards (Seeker Team)

| # | Card Name | Effect |
|---|-----------|--------|
| 1 | **Detour Required** | Hiders must drive to a named landmark (seekers choose from a pre-approved list) within 20 minutes. |
| 2 | **Gravel Road Penalty** | Hiders' next movement must include at least 2 miles of unpaved road. |
| 3 | **County Line Pull** | Hiders must move to within 5 miles of a named county line within 30 minutes. |
| 4 | **Gas Check** | Hiders must announce their current Gas Card balance. |
| 5 | **Direction Lock** | Hiders may only travel in one cardinal direction (seekers choose) for 15 minutes. |
| 6 | **Speed Check** | Hiders must stay under 35 mph for 10 minutes (honor system; intended for back roads). |
| 7 | **Town Requirement** | Hiders must be inside an incorporated town or city limits within 20 minutes. |
| 8 | **Pitstop** | Hiders must stop and park for 10 minutes immediately. |
| 9 | **Open Window** | Hiders must drive with all windows down for 10 minutes (reduces hiding effectiveness in crowds). |
| 10 | **County Reveal** | Hiders must reveal which county they are currently in. |

## Gas Cards

Print these at a smaller size (business card or index card) and use as tokens.

| Card | Mileage Value | Notes |
|------|--------------|-------|
| Quarter Tank | ~100 miles | Most common denomination |
| Half Tank | ~200 miles | |
| Full Tank | ~400 miles | Used at game start |
| Jerrycan | +50 miles | Bonus; awarded by challenges |

*Adjust mileage values to match your vehicle's actual fuel economy.*

## County-Crossing Cards

Print one card per county boundary in your play area. Customize the name:

> **[COUNTY A] ↔ [COUNTY B] Crossing Card**  
> *Play this card to legally cross the boundary between [County A] and [County B].*  
> *Each card is single-use.*
